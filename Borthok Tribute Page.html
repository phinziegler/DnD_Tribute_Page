Achievements

  • Easily defeated Jayce Lightbringer in a 1 versus 1 battle for sport.
  • Has the highest strength stat in the party, which has always embarrassed Jayce.
  • Killed a big scary ghost in a time-travelling mansion in one attack.
  • Tamed an Owlbear after defeating it in unarmed combat.
  • Defeated the vicious "Wolf King" Maurader.
  • Saved an old city when he travelled in the past to defeat a coven of vampires. This is why his likeness is portrayed on a statue in Squadville.
  • Successfully dispatched every attempt by Jayce to use the command spell to make Borthok sit down.

Death

One of Borthok’s only vices was hard liquor. After having lived a long life of battle and debauchery, Borthok struggled in his old age, resorting to liquor to fill the void.

Borthok sought a particular spirit that reminded him of his glory days - “The Brick” it was called.

In his weakened body, Borthok struggled to slurp down that sweet sweet brick juice, and struggled more with the extreme alcohol content of the “beverage,” succumbing to alcohol poisoning in his sleep that night.

Although he did not die a hero's death, he will always be remembered as one in the hearts of those he saved, and the hearts of those he loved.

Intro

Borthok the Unbreakable is a Goliath barbarian born and raised by a village of Golville’s descendents, in the northern mountains. He was a local hero who left his hometown to save the world, defending those who could not defend themselves, and through his shear bravery and willpower carried his party through battle after battle until finally victory was achieved.
